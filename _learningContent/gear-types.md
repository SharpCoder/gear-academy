## Kinds of gears

There are many types of gears out there, and each kind has its own particular use-case. Some notable examples include:

-   Rack and pinion
-   Helical
-   Spur
-   Bevel
-   Worm
-   Miter
-   Screw
-   Internal

In the next section, we'll take a closer look at the Spur Gear and how it is designed.
